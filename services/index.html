Selenium WebDriver Healthcheck

Our experienced consultants can carry out a healthcheck on your current use of Selenium, test infrastructure, and processes to identify issues and recommend practices to maximize your investment in automated testing. The Healthcheck is suited to organisations and projects of any size with the typical time required by our consultants being between 4 hours to 2 days.

The Healthcheck process

  • Onsite visit by one of our experienced consultants over one or two days
  • Inspection of current automated test code that uses Selenium
  • Review of existing Continuous Integration (CI) and test infrastructure
  • Interview current project team members and senior staff
  • Written report containing the identified issues and any appropriate recommendations

What's covered?

  • Identity the cause of brittle tests (Automated tests that randomly fail for no reason) and recommend practices that avoid them.
  • Advise how to implement and migrate to parallel test execution.
  • Configuring CI (Continuous Integration) servers and structuring builds for efficient cross browser testing.
